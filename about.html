In 2021, I obtained my Master's degree at the Faculty of Architecture, KuLeuven. With my thesis, I explored the role of media ecologies in public space/sphere in Warsaw during protests in Poland in 2020/21. Since march 2022, I have been conducting interdisciplinary PhD research in the Faculty of Architecture and the Faculty of Engineering Technology, KULeuven in Gent. In my work, I continue mapping public space through 3D modelling, photogrammetry, scraping, and people's engagement in co-creating collective experiences. I investigates the impact of internet infrastructure on architecture and public life on different scales, from individual use of smartphone devices to digitized representations of cities. Now, I focus on the expansion of strategies and devices that could become a critical design tool for investigation of public spaces. Their development plays an active role in the collective creation of urban spaces.
